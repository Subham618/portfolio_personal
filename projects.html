Agendas

New Juice and Fruit Center Near Old SAC
Refreshments are a must after a tiring and intense game or workout. I plan to set up a new juice and fruit center near Old SAC to make healthy refreshments readily available near the Sports Complex instead of students having to go all the way back to their hostels.
Digitalized Baggage Shelf Token Generation in the Central Library
Losing a Library Token carries with it a heavy fine of 1000 Rs. Replacing the current token system with a digital ID scanning system that records a studentâ€™s details and the corresponding baggage shelf number will eliminate the consequences of accidentally losing the token.
Transparency of fund usage by HAB and HMCs of hostels
Hostel boarders are often unaware of exactly where their hostel's HMC has used funds received from the HAB. Making all hostel transactions public to hostel boarders by the HMC will provide much-needed transparency in the system's working and will allow for checks against corruption
Removal of Late-Night Campus Entry Restrictions
Entry should not be restricted for students reaching campus late at night coming back from Guwahati City
Strict Enforcement of a Speed Limit for all Motored Vehicles on Campus
IITG Buses and trucks often overspeed on narrow and crowded lanes, posing a huge threat to vulnerable cyclists and pedestrians. Enforcing a speed limit will aid in making the campus roads safer for everyone.
Towards a Cleaner Campus
Plastic waste is often found near lakes and tea stalls. Organizing regular cleanliness drives with the help of the student community will aid in the systematic cleaning of several heavily littered areas on campus.
Increase Number and Frequency of E-Rickshaws
Although almost all students generally commute using cycles, E-Rickshaws become an extremely convenient mode of transport when one falls sick. These will also aid commute in the rainy season and for campus visitors
Increase the Number of Branded food outlets
Apart from the average pizza, it would be a great addition to have branded food outlets like Domino's, KFC etc.
A proper portal for attendance tracking
